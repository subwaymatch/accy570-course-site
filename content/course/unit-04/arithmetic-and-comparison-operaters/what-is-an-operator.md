What is an operator? Operators are symbols in Python that performs some sort of an operation.

- Arithmetic operators (plus, minus, multiply, divide)
- Comparison (`=`, `!=`, `>`, `>=`, `<`, `<=`)
- Logical (`and`, `or`)
- Membership operators (`in`, `not in`)
